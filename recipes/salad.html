Salad

Description

Ingredients

  • Mixed salad greens (lettuce, spinach, arugula, etc.)
  • Cherry tomatoes (halved)
  • Cucumber (sliced)
  • Red onion (thinly sliced)
  • Carrots (shredded or sliced)
  • Bell pepper (sliced)
  • Olives (optional)
  • Feta cheese or shredded cheese (optional)
  • Croutons (optional)
  • Salad dressing (vinaigrette, ranch, etc.)

Steps

  1. Wash and dry all the salad greens and vegetables thoroughly.
  2. Chop or slice the vegetables as desired (tomatoes, cucumber, onion, carrots, bell pepper, etc.).
  3. In a large bowl, combine the salad greens with the chopped vegetables.
  4. Add any optional ingredients like olives, cheese, or croutons.
  5. Toss the salad gently to mix everything evenly.
  6. Drizzle your favorite dressing over the salad.
  7. Toss again lightly to coat the salad with the dressing.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy!
